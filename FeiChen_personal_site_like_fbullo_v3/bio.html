Biography

Fei Chen is the Principal Investigator of the Laboratory for Information and Control Systems (LICS) at the College of AI, Nankai University (Tianjin, China).

Research interests include distributed cooperative control, distributed optimization, and distributed learning, with emphasis on networked and multi-agent systems.
